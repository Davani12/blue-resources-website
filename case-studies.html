Classm8: Personalizing Education with AI

2022-2023 EdTech Thailand Coming in 2025

Overview

Classm8 is our flagship EdTech application, designed to personalize learning for students. Using AI, it adapts to individual learning styles, tracks progress, and boosts engagement. In pilot schools across Thailand, Classm8 has improved student performance by 30%.

Early Access Opportunity: ClassM8 is launching to the public in the second half of 2025. We're currently recruiting users for case studies and offering early adopters special benefits including priority access and a 30% discount. Sign up below to join our early access program.

The Challenge

Traditional classrooms struggle to meet diverse student needs, leading to disengagement and uneven learning outcomes.

  • High student-to-teacher ratios limiting personalized attention
  • Diverse learning styles and paces among students
  • Limited data about student performance and engagement
  • Difficulty in adapting curriculum to individual needs

They approached Blue Resources to develop a solution that could personalize learning at scale while providing actionable insights to educators.

The Solution

Classm8 uses AI to tailor education to each student. It analyzes learning patterns, curates content, and provides real-time feedback, empowering students and teachers alike.

  • Adaptive Learning Paths: Personalized content sequencing based on individual student performance and learning styles
  • Real-time Assessment: Continuous evaluation of comprehension with adaptive difficulty levels
  • Engagement Analytics: Detailed insights on student interaction and attention levels
  • Natural Language Interface: Conversational AI that responds to student questions and provides explanations
  • Teacher Dashboard: Comprehensive analytics and intervention suggestions for educators

Implementation Process

  1. Research and Design: Extensive user research with students and teachers to understand needs
  2. Algorithm Development: Creation of proprietary learning models based on educational psychology
  3. Prototype Testing: Iterative testing with focus groups to refine functionality
  4. Pilot Program: Three-month trial with select classrooms
  5. Full Deployment: Rollout across the institution with ongoing support

The Impact

  • 30% improvement in student performance in Thai pilot schools
  • 15% reduction in dropout rates due to higher engagement
  • Teachers equipped with actionable insights
