Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the ClassM8 Early Access Program

When exactly will ClassM8 launch?

ClassM8 is scheduled for public launch in the second half of 2025. Early access participants will get access to the platform approximately 6 months before the public launch.

Who can join the early access program?

The program is open to educational institutions, teachers, students, and education technology professionals. We're looking for diverse participants to help us test and refine the platform.

What is required from early access participants?

We ask participants to use the platform regularly and provide feedback through surveys and occasional interviews. Your input will help us improve ClassM8 before the public launch.

How long does the early access program last?

The program will run for approximately 6 months before the public launch. After the launch, early access participants will transition to regular users with their exclusive benefits intact.

Is there a cost to join the early access program?

The early access program itself is free. When ClassM8 launches publicly, early access participants will receive a 30% discount on subscriptions for the first two years.

How do I apply for the early access program?

Simply fill out the sign-up form on this page with your email address. Our team will contact you with more information and next steps.
