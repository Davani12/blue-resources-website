About Blue Resources

Who We Are

Founded in 2023 in Bangkok, Thailand, Blue Resources Co., Ltd. emerged from a passion for artificial intelligence and a vision to lead AI innovation in Southeast Asia. Starting as a small team of AI enthusiasts, we've grown into a dynamic company delivering impactful solutions across industries like education, healthcare, and finance. Our commitment to research, collaboration, and education drives everything we do.

Our Mission

We aim to pioneer AI for a connected future by providing innovative solutions, comprehensive education, and strategic partnerships that empower our clients and communities.

Our Vision

We see a Southeast Asia where AI is accessible to all, fostering innovation, creating opportunities, and building a smarter, more connected region.

Our Values

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible with AI, exploring new approaches and technologies.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and collaborative efforts to solve complex problems.

Ethics

We develop AI with strong ethical standards, ensuring responsible use and positive societal impact.

Human-Centered

Our AI solutions are designed with people at the center, enhancing human capabilities rather than replacing them.
